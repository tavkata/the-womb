Duncan Greenstrings (born October 7, 1968) is a seminal British music figure. Adored as the falsetto-vocalist and songwriter in the critically acclaimed alt rock band The Womb, as well as for his strong political stances and solo work, Greenstrings originates out of London, England. Due to onstage trilateral frequencies, the stadium floor has been known to collapse on more than one occassion.
Konstantine Kuchev is a Bulgarian violinist and violist. He made his early career in the classical field, and has more recently performed in jazz, klezmer and other music genres. He posseses a maverick sensibility that enables him to move expertly between the jazz, rock and classical music worlds. It has been said that he is a musical force to be reckoned with. You can decide for yourself.
Tavis Preston (born September 11th 1983) is a Scottish double bass player and composer. He is best known as the bass player from the Womb and The Bedlam Club and currently resides in Sofia, Bulgaria. His profound disposition and capabity has enticed the likes of royalty Prince Charles and Lady Gaga alongside performances in front of 100,000 welcomers on the shores of Africa 2016.
